MAJA Branding

MAJA is an artisanal ice cream brand inspired by the rich cultural heritage of the Lenca ethnic group from Honduras and El Salvador. This project celebrates Lenca crafts, drawing design inspiration from their intricate textiles and ceramics. The packaging reflects the vibrant colors, patterns, and traditions of the Lenca people.
