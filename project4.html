Target Cards

This project features a set of six cards: three gift cards and three matching greeting cards for the themes of Happy Birthday, Congratulations, and Thank You. Drawing inspiration from Target's illustrative and vibrant brand identity, each design incorporates playful elements and a cohesive style that resonates with the brand's fun and approachable aesthetic.
